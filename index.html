Philosophy: What do I mean by:

"where fun and practical meet music"

Fun

What is music without some fun in it? Among the many things I do in a lesson, my top priorities are to help students find fascination and inspiration in their instruments and music!

Practical

There is deeper meaning when we know not just the what, but the why and the how. My students will be able to apply everything they learn immediately by making real music.

Music

I believe that music happens when a student finds magic in the sounds they make and even more magic when sharing that music with friends and family and collaborating with other musicians. We do this through solo performances and jam sessions!

What do I teach in my holistic approach?

As both a musician and teacher, I approach music holistically. There is much more to making music beyond playing your notes and singing your songs. I am a strong proponent of developing my students into versatile musicians. This way, I believe, you can have the most fun doing music!

Music Theory

Build strong foundational knowledge. Learn, use, adapt!

Musicality and Expression

Learn to hear the emotions of music. Then speak it.

Posture

Save yourself from muscle overuse and injuries. I’ll keep reminding you!

Sightreading

Jealous of people who can instantly play music? That’ll be you soon!

Technique

Exercises so that you can play challenging music with ease.

Ear Training

Jealous of people who know the chords by ear? That’ll be you soon!

Composition

Apply your theoretical knowledge and write your own music!

Improvisation

Have fun. A lot of fun. Many funs. You’re gonna love this.

Collaborative Arts

Communicate non-verbally and know when to shine or support.

Things I teach

Guitar

Among the many styles of guitar playing, my specialty is the classical and acoustic guitar! These are great places to start since they give students all the basics required to branch out to other styles!

Beginner's Piano

The piano is great platform for anyone getting started with music. I use the Robert Pace Piano Method. This method helps students develop deep and flexible piano skills that allows them to sightread and sight-transpose right in their first lessons.

Flute

I have a wide experience with the flute, playing with various ensembles across the world. Though I specialize in classical flute playing, I have also performed extensively in pop and acoustic styles.

Music Theory

Master the building blocks of music and turn this knowledge into fun compositions. I integrate music theory into all my lessons but you can choose to dig deeper together.
